mise en place de la politique de crise et permettre les choix des ministres et critères :
6.2 Crisis mode — The twelve voices
A situation is declared an emergency when at least two of the following four criteria are met,
including necessarily the irreversible risk or critical collapse criterion:
• Immediate systemic impact: more than 20% of the population directly affected
• Irreversible risk: the situation cannot be corrected after the action deadline
• Critical action deadline of less than 30 days
• Collapse of a vital function: water, energy, health or security
Three ministers are designated by the constitution to validate the triggering of an emergency —
chosen for their most analytical and structured characters: the Analyst, the Strategist and the
Arbiter.
Three ministers are designated to propose the end of the crisis: the Guardian (resources
restored?), the Protector (return of sense of security?), the Guide (clear and shared exit vision?).

sécesion :
7.2 The assisted secession protocol
CONDITION 1 — THE NEGOTIATION PERIOD
Before any formal secession, a mandatory negotiation period is opened (typically between three
and twelve months depending on the size of the group). This period allows for an attempt to revise
the founding prompts that might make the secession unnecessary.
CONDITION 2 — THE NON-AGGRESSION TREATY
Before receiving departure assistance, the secessionist group signs a non-aggression treaty with
the original entity. This treaty defines borders, shared resources, rules of coexistence and
mechanisms for resolving future conflicts
